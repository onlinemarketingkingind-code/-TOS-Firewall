The Process

Not one AI.
Four independent auditors.

Every other ToS tool runs a single AI model. A single model can hallucinate. A single model has biases. A single model's error is your liability. TOS Firewall runs four independent engines simultaneously — Claude, Gemini, GPT-4o, and Grok. A clause is only flagged when multiple systems agree.

1
Submit your ToS document
Paste your Terms of Service URL or text. Our extractor pulls the full legal document.
2
Four engines audit simultaneously
Claude, Gemini, GPT-4o, and Grok (xAI) each independently analyze your document across 6 legal categories aligned with GDPR, CCPA, and India's DPDPA 2023.
3
Consensus scoring
A conflict resolution layer compares all three outputs. Disagreements are escalated. Only consensus findings appear in your report.
4
Embed your verified badge
One line of code. Your trust badge appears on your signup page — live, updated automatically when your ToS changes.
